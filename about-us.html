About US

River Retail is a family run business that has several years of experience managing and selling on Amazon. We are your Amazon Marketplace experts. We specialize in helping companies gain control over their brands on the Amazon Marketplace. We work with you to understand your business to enhance your Amazon presence.

We have worked closely with our sister company to develop custom software to help us manage products, generate reports, track issues, and research problems so we can get them fixed quickly. We are committed to professionally managing your brand and increasing sales for your products. We have reliable and professionally trained staff that monitor and enhance product listings daily.
